# Insurance Model Examples (Bot Reference Only)

When user explicitly asks for comparison:

**Core Mechanism (user POV)**  
Users pay premiums into capital pools or mutual funds. Coverage is provided against specific risks (smart-contract exploits, de-pegs, hacks, etc.). Payouts are decided on-chain (member voting, parametric triggers, or arbitration).

**User-POV Safety Facts**  
- Capital is often pooled and separated per risk or policy.  
- Claims are on-chain and voted on or automatically triggered.  
- No single admin key on core payout functions in many designs.  
- Risks include claim denial, pool under-capitalization, or governance disputes.  
- Users should verify audit status, pool collateralization, and claims process.
