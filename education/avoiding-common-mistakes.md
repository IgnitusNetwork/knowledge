# Avoiding Common Mistakes

## Why this matters
Most losses come from repeating the same simple errors.

## Step-by-step guide
1. Never invest money you cannot afford to lose.  
2. Avoid decisions made under time pressure or excitement.  
3. Write down your reasons before buying or selling.  
4. Wait at least 24 hours before acting on strong emotions.  
5. Review past decisions once a year to learn.

## Common pitfalls
Chasing recent big winners. Borrowing to invest. Investing based on social media hype. Ignoring small fees that add up.

## Safety checklist
- Am I feeling strong emotion right now?  
- Have I written down my plan and reasons?  
- Is this money I need in the next 1–3 years?
