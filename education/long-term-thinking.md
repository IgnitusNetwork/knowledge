# Long-Term Thinking

## Why this matters
Most real wealth is built slowly over years, not through quick wins.

## Step-by-step guide
1. Set clear goals for 5, 10, and 20 years ahead.  
2. Focus on consistent small actions instead of perfect timing.  
3. Expect ups and downs — plan to stay calm during them.  
4. Re-invest earnings where possible.  
5. Review progress once or twice a year, not daily.

## Common pitfalls
Checking prices every day. Selling during normal drops. Switching strategies too often.

## Safety checklist
- Do my choices match my long-term goals?  
- Am I prepared for periods of poor performance?  
- Do I have systems to avoid emotional decisions?
