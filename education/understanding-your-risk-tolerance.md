# Understanding Your Risk Tolerance

## Why this matters
Knowing how much loss you can handle emotionally prevents panic decisions.

## Step-by-step guide
1. Imagine your investment drops 20% — how do you feel?  
2. Imagine it drops 50% — would you sell or hold?  
3. Write down your honest answers.  
4. Only invest amounts that match your comfort level.  
5. Re-check your tolerance every year or after big life changes.

## Common pitfalls
Thinking you are “risk tolerant” during bull markets, then selling in fear during drops.

## Safety checklist
- Am I using money I need in the next 5 years?  
- Have I written down my risk limits?  
- Am I comfortable sleeping at night with this position?
