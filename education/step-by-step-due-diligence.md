# Step-by-Step Due Diligence

## Why this matters
A simple checklist can prevent most big mistakes.

## Step-by-step guide
1. Read the basic description and rules.  
2. Check on-chain data if available.  
3. Verify key addresses and balances.  
4. Look for red flags.  
5. Ask: What is the worst-case scenario?  
6. Start with a very small amount.

## Common pitfalls
Skipping steps because of FOMO or nice marketing.

## Safety checklist
- Can I explain how it works in my own words?  
- Have I verified the most important facts myself?  
- Do I have an exit plan?
