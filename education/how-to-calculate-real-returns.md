# How to Calculate Real Returns

## Why this matters
Headline numbers often hide the real gain after fees, inflation, and taxes.

## Step-by-step guide
1. Start with final amount received.  
2. Subtract all fees paid.  
3. Subtract inflation effect.  
4. Compare to what you would have earned doing nothing.  
5. Repeat for different time periods.

## Common pitfalls
Only looking at gross returns. Ignoring compounding effect of fees.

## Safety checklist
- Have I included all fees and costs?  
- Did I adjust for inflation?  
- Is the real return worth the risk taken?
