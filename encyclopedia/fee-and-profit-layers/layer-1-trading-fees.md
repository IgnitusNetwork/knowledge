# Layer 1: Trading Fees — The First Cut

## What it is
A direct percentage taken from every buy or sell transaction.

## What happens to your money (user POV)
Part of your money is removed before it even enters the position (or before you receive the proceeds). A round-trip (buy then sell) can remove 2% or more even if the price does not move.

## Common ways users get hurt
Frequent trading multiplies the loss. Over time your starting capital is permanently reduced.

## Simple example with numbers
You start with $1,000.  
Buy: 1% fee → $990 enters.  
Sell: 1% fee → you receive about $980.  
You lost $20 before any price change.

## What to check (safety checklist)
- What is the exact fee percentage for buy and sell?  
- How often do I plan to trade?  
- Does the fee apply to the full amount every time?
