# Layer 6: Hidden Costs in Yield and Rewards

## What it is
Extra expenses and risks hidden inside “high yield” or reward offers.

## What happens to your money (user POV)
You see attractive returns, but fees, inflation, taxes, or sudden rule changes quietly remove much of the benefit.

## Common ways users get hurt
The headline yield looks good, but after all hidden costs your real return is low or negative.

## Simple example with numbers
Advertised 12% yield.  
After fees, inflation, and occasional losses you actually keep 3–4% — or lose money in bad periods.

## What to check (safety checklist)
- What are all the costs subtracted from the yield?  
- How stable has this yield been in the past?  
- What risks could suddenly reduce or stop the rewards?
