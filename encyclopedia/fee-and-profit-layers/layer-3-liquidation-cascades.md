# Layer 3: Liquidation Cascades

## What it is
A chain reaction where forced sales push prices down further, triggering more forced sales.

## What happens to your money (user POV)
Your position can be automatically sold at the worst possible moment, often at a big loss, even if you planned to hold longer.

## Common ways users get hurt
Borrowed positions get closed suddenly. Prices drop faster than you can react.

## Simple example with numbers
You borrow to buy $10,000 worth of an asset.  
Price drops 15%.  
The system sells everything at the bottom to protect the lender.  
You lose most of your original money plus extra fees.

## What to check (safety checklist)
- How much price drop would trigger liquidation?  
- What are the exact rules and warning systems?  
- Do I have extra buffer to avoid forced sales?
