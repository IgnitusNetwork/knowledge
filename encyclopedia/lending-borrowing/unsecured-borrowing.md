# Unsecured Borrowing (Loans with No Collateral)

## What it is
Borrowing money based only on your promise to repay — no house or car is offered as security.

## What happens to your money (user POV)
You get the money faster and with less paperwork, but interest rates are much higher. The lender relies only on your income and credit history.

## Common ways users get hurt
Higher interest makes the total cost much larger. Late payments damage your future borrowing ability and add heavy fees.

## Simple example with numbers
You borrow $8,000 unsecured.  
Interest rate 18% over 4 years.  
You repay about $11,800 in total.  
The extra $3,800 is the real cost of the loan.

## What to check (safety checklist)
- What is the full amount I will repay including all interest?  
- What happens if I miss payments?  
- Is there a cheaper secured option available?
