# Lending as an Investor (Earning Interest on Your Money)

## What it is
Putting your money into lending opportunities so you earn interest over time.

## What happens to your money (user POV)
Your money is given to borrowers. You receive regular interest payments. At the end you should get your original money back.

## Common ways users get hurt
Borrowers may not repay (default). Your money can be locked for long periods. Inflation can reduce the real value of your returns.

## Simple example with numbers
You lend $10,000 at 7% per year.  
You receive $700 interest each year.  
After inflation at 3%, your real gain is only about $400 per year.

## What to check (safety checklist)
- How safe is the borrower?  
- Can I get my money back early if I need it?  
- What fees does the lending platform or service take?
