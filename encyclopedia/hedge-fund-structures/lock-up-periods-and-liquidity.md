# Lock-up Periods and Liquidity

## What it is
The time during which you cannot take your money out of the fund.

## What happens to your money (user POV)
Your money is committed for months or years. Even in an emergency you may not be able to access it.

## Common ways users get hurt
You need cash but cannot get it. In bad markets you are forced to stay invested while losses grow.

## Simple example with numbers
One-year lock-up.  
You invest in January.  
You cannot withdraw until next January even if the fund is losing money or you have a personal emergency.

## What to check (safety checklist)
- How long is the lock-up period?  
- Are there any early withdrawal penalties?  
- How often can I normally request my money?
