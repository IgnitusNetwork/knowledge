# Leverage and Risk Taking

## What it is
Using borrowed money to increase the size of investments.

## What happens to your money (user POV)
Gains and losses are magnified. Small market moves can create big wins or wipe out large parts of your investment.

## Common ways users get hurt
Leverage turns normal market drops into major losses. Many funds have collapsed because of too much borrowing.

## Simple example with numbers
$1 million investment with 3x leverage = $3 million exposure.  
A 10% market drop loses $300,000 (30% of your money) instead of $100,000.

## What to check (safety checklist)
- How much leverage does the fund use?  
- What happens in a sudden market drop?  
- Are there limits on how much risk they can take?
