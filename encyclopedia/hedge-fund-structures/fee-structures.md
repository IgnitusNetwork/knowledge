# Fee Structures in Hedge Funds

## What it is
The different charges the fund takes from your investment for managing and performing.

## What happens to your money (user POV)
Every year a portion of your money is taken automatically as management fees plus extra if the fund makes profit.

## Common ways users get hurt
Fees continue even when the fund loses money. Over many years they eat a large part of your total return.

## Simple example with numbers
You invest $1 million.  
2% management fee = $20,000 every year regardless of performance.  
20% performance fee on profits further reduces what you keep.

## What to check (safety checklist)
- What is the exact management fee percentage?  
- When and how is the performance fee calculated?  
- Are there any hidden or extra charges?
