# Red Flags Checklist

## What it is
Warning signs that suggest higher risk or possible problems.

## What happens to your money (user POV)
Spotting these early can help you avoid or reduce losses.

## Common ways users get hurt
Ignoring red flags because of excitement or pressure from others.

## Simple example with numbers
A promise of “guaranteed 20% per month” with no clear explanation.  
Many people lose their full investment when it collapses.

## What to check (safety checklist)
- Are returns promised as guaranteed or very high with low risk?  
- Is there pressure to invest quickly?  
- Are the people involved transparent about how it works?  
- Can I easily find clear, honest explanations?  
- Do I understand exactly where my money is going?
