# Due Diligence Basics

## What it is
The basic steps you take to check something before putting your money in.

## What happens to your money (user POV)
Spending a few hours checking can prevent big losses later.

## Common ways users get hurt
Relying only on nice promises or what friends say without doing your own checks.

## Simple example with numbers
You spend 2 hours reading documents and checking numbers.  
This small effort helps you avoid a product that later loses 70% of its value.

## What to check (safety checklist)
- Can I find clear, honest explanations?  
- Are the numbers and rules easy to understand?  
- Can I verify key claims myself?  
- What do I still not understand?
