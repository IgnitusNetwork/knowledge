# Understanding Risk

## What it is
Risk is the chance that you will lose some or all of your money, or that your money will not grow as expected.

## What happens to your money (user POV)
Every place you put money has some risk. Higher possible returns usually come with higher chance of loss. Understanding this helps you make calmer decisions.

## Common ways users get hurt
People underestimate risk when things are going well, then get shocked when losses happen. They also chase high returns without realising how much they can lose.

## Simple example with numbers
You put $10,000 in something “safe” that promises 8% return.  
In a bad period it drops 30%.  
You now have $7,000 and need a 43% gain just to get back to where you started.

## What to check (safety checklist)
- What is the realistic worst-case loss?  
- How would I feel if I lost 20%, 50%, or 100%?  
- Do I need this money soon?
