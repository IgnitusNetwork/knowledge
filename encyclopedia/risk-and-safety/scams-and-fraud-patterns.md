# Scams and Fraud Patterns

## What it is
Common tricks used to take people’s money.

## What happens to your money (user POV)
Scammers use trust, urgency, and fake promises to make you send money that is never returned.

## Common ways users get hurt
Fake opportunities that sound too good, pressure to act fast, or requests for money upfront.

## Simple example with numbers
Someone promises to double your money in weeks.  
You send $5,000.  
You never see it again.

## What to check (safety checklist)
- Does it sound too good to be true?  
- Is there pressure to decide quickly?  
- Can I easily get my money back if needed?
