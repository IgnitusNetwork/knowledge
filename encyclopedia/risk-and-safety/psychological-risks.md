# Psychological Risks

## What it is
The dangers that come from how our own minds behave with money.

## What happens to your money (user POV)
Fear, greed, and excitement cause people to buy high, sell low, or take bigger risks than planned.

## Common ways users get hurt
Following the crowd, panic selling during drops, or chasing recent winners.

## Simple example with numbers
Everyone is excited and prices are high → you buy.  
Prices fall sharply → you sell in fear.  
You bought high and sold low, locking in the loss.

## What to check (safety checklist)
- Do I have a written plan I can stick to?  
- Am I making this decision because of emotion or logic?  
- Can I wait 24–48 hours before big moves?
