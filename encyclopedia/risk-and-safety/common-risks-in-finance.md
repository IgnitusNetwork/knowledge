# Common Risks in Finance

## What it is
The main types of risk that affect normal people’s money.

## What happens to your money (user POV)
Your savings or investments can lose value because of market changes, inflation, company problems, or bigger economic events.

## Common ways users get hurt
- Market risk: prices fall suddenly  
- Inflation risk: money buys less over time  
- Credit risk: the borrower does not repay  
- Liquidity risk: you cannot sell when you need to

## Simple example with numbers
Inflation at 5% per year.  
Your savings earn 2%.  
After 5 years your money has lost real purchasing power even though the number in your account grew slightly.

## What to check (safety checklist)
- Which risks matter most for my situation?  
- How does this choice behave in bad markets?  
- Do I have protection against the biggest risks?
