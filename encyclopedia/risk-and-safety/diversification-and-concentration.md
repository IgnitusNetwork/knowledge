# Diversification and Concentration

## What it is
Spreading money across different things (diversification) versus putting most of it in one place (concentration).

## What happens to your money (user POV)
Diversification reduces the chance of big losses. Concentration can create big wins but also total wipeouts.

## Common ways users get hurt
Putting too much in one thing that suddenly fails.

## Simple example with numbers
$10,000 all in one asset that drops 80% → you have $2,000 left.  
$10,000 split across 10 assets, one drops 80% → you still have about $9,200.

## What to check (safety checklist)
- How much of my total money is in one place?  
- Do I understand all the things I am invested in?  
- Am I comfortable with the risk level?
