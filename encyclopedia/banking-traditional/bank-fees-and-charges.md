# Bank Fees and Charges

## What it is
Extra amounts the bank takes for using their services — beyond the interest they earn.

## What happens to your money (user POV)
Every time you do certain things (overdraft, ATM outside the network, paper statements, international transfers) the bank deducts a fee. These come straight out of your account.

## Common ways users get hurt
Small fees add up to hundreds of dollars per year without you noticing. They hit hardest when your balance is already low.

## Simple example with numbers
- $35 overdraft fee (once)  
- $12 monthly maintenance fee (12 months)  
- $3 ATM fee × 8 times  
= $191 lost in one year for normal banking activities.

## What to check (safety checklist)
- What are all the possible fees and when do they apply?  
- Is there a way to avoid them (higher balance, direct deposit, etc.)?  
- Can I get a fee-free account option?
