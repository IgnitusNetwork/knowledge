# What is Debt

## What it is
Debt is simply owing money to someone else with the promise to pay it back, usually with extra (interest).

## What happens to your money (user POV)
You get money or goods now, but you must repay more later. On a bigger scale, governments and companies do the same thing — they borrow from investors and promise to repay with interest.

## Common ways users get hurt
Debt can feel easy at first but grows heavier over time. Too much debt limits your choices and creates constant pressure to make payments.

## Simple example with numbers
You buy a phone on a payment plan for $800.  
You pay $50 per month for 18 months = $900 total.  
The extra $100 is the cost of using the phone before you fully paid for it.

## What to check (safety checklist)
- How much extra will I pay in total?  
- Can I afford the regular payments even if life gets harder?  
- What happens if I cannot repay on time?
