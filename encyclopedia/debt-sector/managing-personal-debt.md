# Managing Personal Debt

## What it is
Practical ways ordinary people handle and reduce what they owe.

## What happens to your money (user POV)
Making a clear plan, paying high-interest debt first, and avoiding new borrowing helps regain control.

## Common ways users get hurt
Ignoring debt makes it grow. Paying only minimum amounts keeps you in debt for decades.

## Simple example with numbers
You have two debts: $5,000 credit card at 22% and $15,000 car loan at 7%.  
Paying extra on the credit card first saves you thousands in interest.

## What to check (safety checklist)
- List all debts with interest rates.  
- Can I cut spending to pay more than the minimum?  
- Should I combine debts or talk to the lender?
