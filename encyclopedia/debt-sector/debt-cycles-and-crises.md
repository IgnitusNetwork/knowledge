# Debt Cycles and Crises

## What it is
Periods when debt builds up for many years, then suddenly becomes hard to manage, leading to big problems in the economy.

## What happens to your money (user POV)
During good times people and governments borrow more. When times turn bad, repayments become difficult, leading to job losses, higher prices, or cuts in services.

## Common ways users get hurt
Ordinary people lose jobs, see savings lose value, or face higher taxes exactly when money is tightest.

## Simple example with numbers
Debt builds for 10 years during easy times.  
Then interest rates rise or income drops.  
Many borrowers cannot pay → banks tighten lending → businesses shrink → people lose work.

## What to check (safety checklist)
- Are debt levels rising much faster than income?  
- Are there signs of over-borrowing in the economy?  
- Do I have personal savings or backup plans for hard times?
