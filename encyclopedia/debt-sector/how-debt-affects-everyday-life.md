# How Debt Affects Everyday Life

## What it is
The real impact of large-scale debt on normal people’s daily finances.

## What happens to your money (user POV)
High national or company debt often leads to higher prices, lower wage growth, more expensive loans, and less government support when you need it.

## Common ways users get hurt
You pay more for everything while your income grows slower. Future opportunities (housing, education, retirement) become harder to reach.

## Simple example with numbers
National debt leads to higher taxes and inflation at 4%.  
Your salary rises 2%.  
Each year you fall further behind in real buying power.

## What to check (safety checklist)
- How is inflation affecting my savings and wages?  
- Are my loans fixed-rate or will payments rise?  
- Am I building enough personal savings outside the system?
