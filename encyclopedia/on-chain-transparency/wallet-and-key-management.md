# Wallet and Key Management

## What it is
Safely controlling the private keys that give access to your on-chain assets.

## What happens to your money (user POV)
If you lose or expose your private keys, you permanently lose access to your money. Good management protects it.

## Common ways users get hurt
Storing keys on phones or computers connected to the internet. Sharing seed phrases. Losing the backup.

## Simple example with numbers
You write your 12-word recovery phrase on paper and store it safely offline.  
Even if your computer is hacked, your money stays protected.

## What to check (safety checklist)
- Do I keep my seed phrase completely offline?  
- Have I tested recovering a wallet with a small test amount?  
- Do I use hardware wallets for larger amounts?  
- Do I never share my private keys or seed phrase?
