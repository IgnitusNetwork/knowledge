# Limitations of On-Chain Transparency

## What it is
What on-chain visibility cannot show you.

## What happens to your money (user POV)
You can see what happened, but not always why, or what will happen next, or what is happening off-chain.

## Common ways users get hurt
Believing that visible = safe. Many important risks remain hidden.

## Simple example with numbers
You see $10 million in an address, but you cannot see if the owner has secretly promised that money to someone else off-chain.

## What to check (safety checklist)
- What important information is still not visible on-chain?  
- Do I understand what I cannot verify?  
- Am I combining on-chain checks with other safety steps?
