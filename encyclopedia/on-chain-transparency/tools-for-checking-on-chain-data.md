# Tools for Checking On-Chain Data

## What it is
Free public websites and apps that let you view blockchain information.

## What happens to your money (user POV)
You open a website, paste an address or transaction ID, and see the details in a more readable format.

## Common ways users get hurt
Using untrusted tools that show wrong information or steal your data.

## Simple example with numbers
You paste a contract address into a well-known public explorer and instantly see all transactions and current balances.

## What to check (safety checklist)
- Am I using well-known, widely trusted explorers?  
- Do I cross-check the same information on two different tools?  
- Do I never enter my private keys on any website?
