# What is On-Chain Transparency

## What it is
Information that is permanently recorded on a public digital ledger that anyone can view.

## What happens to your money (user POV)
Every transfer, deposit, or withdrawal that happens on-chain is visible to the world. You can check exactly what happened, when, and how much — without needing permission from any company.

## Common ways users get hurt
People assume everything on-chain is automatically safe. Visibility does not mean safety — bad actions can still be fully visible.

## Simple example with numbers
You send $1,000 to an address.  
Anyone in the world can see the exact amount, time, and addresses involved forever.

## What to check (safety checklist)
- Is the important information actually recorded on-chain?  
- Can I easily find and understand the records?  
- Do I verify things myself instead of trusting others?
