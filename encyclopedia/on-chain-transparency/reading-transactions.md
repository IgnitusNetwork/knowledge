# Reading Transactions

## What it is
Looking at the public record of a single transfer or action on the blockchain.

## What happens to your money (user POV)
You can see exactly where money went, how much, and any extra details attached to the transaction.

## Common ways users get hurt
Not understanding what a transaction actually did until after it is too late.

## Simple example with numbers
You see a transaction for $5,000 leaving your wallet.  
You check and discover it went to a contract you did not intend.  
You learn to double-check before confirming future transactions.

## What to check (safety checklist)
- What is the exact amount and destination?  
- Are there any extra instructions (contract calls)?  
- Does the transaction match what I expected?
