# Checking Balances and Backing

## What it is
Verifying how much money or assets are actually held in a system.

## What happens to your money (user POV)
You can look up the public balances yourself instead of relying only on what someone tells you.

## Common ways users get hurt
Assuming the advertised backing amount is correct without checking.

## Simple example with numbers
A system claims $10 million in backing.  
You check the public addresses and see only $6 million is actually there.  
You discover the difference before committing more money.

## What to check (safety checklist)
- Are the official reserve addresses public?  
- Do the numbers match what is claimed?  
- How often do the balances change?
